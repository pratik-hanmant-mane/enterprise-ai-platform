# Enterprise AI Platform

## Vision

Enterprise AI Platform is a production-grade backend platform that demonstrates modern enterprise software engineering, cloud-native architecture, and AI-powered business services.

The project is being developed as a long-term portfolio application that evolves from a modular monolith into a scalable enterprise platform. Its primary goal is to showcase best practices in backend development, distributed systems, DevOps, cloud infrastructure, and enterprise AI integration.

---

# Objectives

* Build production-ready REST APIs using FastAPI.
* Apply Clean Architecture and Domain-Driven Design principles.
* Design secure authentication and role-based authorization.
* Implement scalable database and caching strategies.
* Integrate AI capabilities into enterprise workflows.
* Deploy using modern cloud-native infrastructure.
* Demonstrate observability, testing, and DevOps practices.

---

# Problem Statement

Organizations often rely on multiple disconnected business systems such as ERP, CRM, HRMS, document management systems, and third-party services. Employees spend significant time searching for information, performing repetitive tasks, and manually coordinating workflows.

The Enterprise AI Platform provides a centralized backend capable of integrating these systems while enabling intelligent automation, secure APIs, and AI-powered business assistants.
